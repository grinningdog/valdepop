About U3A Vall del Pop

Learning, friendship, and enjoyment in retirement

Who We Are

U3A Vall del Pop is part of the worldwide University of the Third Age movement, providing educational and social opportunities for people in their 'third age' of life - typically those who are retired or semi-retired.

Our Mission

We aim to provide a supportive environment where members can share their knowledge, skills, and experiences while learning from others. Our philosophy is based on self-help learning - members teaching members.

What We Offer

  • Wide range of interest groups and activities
  • Cultural trips and excursions
  • Social events and gatherings
  • Opportunities to make new friends
  • Mental and physical stimulation

Join Us

We welcome new members throughout the year. Whether you're a long-term resident or new to the area, U3A Vall del Pop offers a warm and friendly community.
