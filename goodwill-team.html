Welcome to the Goodwill Team

The Goodwill team, the charity fundraising arm of the u3a Vall del Pop, hold regular fundraising events for the enjoyment of members and to raise funds for local charities.

These can include:

  • Car boot sales
  • Quizzes
  • Social events
  • Craft days
  • Music events
  • BBQs
  • Afternoon teas etc.

The monies raised on an annual basis are shared amongst selected local charities at year end.

Events are advertised on the website and in the monthly newsletter and can be booked and paid for at the monthly General Assemblies and coffee mornings. To contact the Goodwill Team click the button below.
