"The purpose of the u3a is to encourage life-long learning for people who are no longer in full time employment."

The u3a Vall del Pop is a self-help organisation for people no longer in full-time employment. We offer a wide range of learning activities and social events for our members. Our aim is to provide a friendly and supportive environment where members can share their knowledge and skills, learn new things, and make new friends.

We have a diverse range of activity groups, from art and crafts to walking and dancing. We also organise regular social events, trips, and outings. Whether you are looking to learn a new skill, stay active, or simply meet new people, the u3a Vall del Pop has something for everyone.
